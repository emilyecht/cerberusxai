Evidence boundary

What this interface does—and refuses to claim.

Defensible here

  • Explanatory fixed-seed synthetic traces
  • Transparent residual-correlation and Fisher-bound calculations
  • Illustrations of demotion, dwell, hysteresis, and unknown handling
  • Exact cut-set overlap and separate near-match reporting

Not established

  • Operational detector performance or realistic telemetry robustness
  • Causal discovery, full probabilistic FCOI, or complete ontologies
  • Flight authority validity, certification, TRL, or a completed safety case
  • Sentinel safety or graph-external witness independence
