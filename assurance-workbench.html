      Evidence boundary

      This workbench makes decisions inspectable. It does not make them certified.

      Shown: illustrative authority logic, one-way evidence effects, explicit provenance rules, traceable demotion, sequential recovery, negative controls, executable reference code, and a bounded research roadmap.

      Not established: operational detector performance, realistic telemetry robustness, complete causal models, flight authority validity, hardware isolation, certification, TRL, or a completed safety case.

      Evidence may reduce permission. It cannot manufacture authority.
