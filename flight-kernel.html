    Load-bearing properties

    Small enough to challenge.

    The C11 reference kernel isolates the authority law from orbital dynamics, authentication, and FCOI estimation so its core state transitions remain inspectable.

    Demote immediately

    A lower defensible target is applied in the same step and may skip intermediate states.

    P3

    Restore sequentially

    Fresh evidence must persist through dwell; promotion advances by only one state.

    P4

    Reject replay

    A repeated evidence epoch cannot fill the recovery counter.

    P5

    Non-delegable boundary

    Assign-authority and override-Anchor are denied at every state.

    What this establishes

    An inspectable implementation can enforce the intended authority asymmetry.

    Implemented: C11 authority kernel, named unit tests, deterministic randomized property campaign, CBMC harness, process-separated worker topology, capability-scoped command path, freshness/epoch handling, and explicit A0 action residue.

    Not established: correct mission thresholds, realistic detector performance, spacecraft dynamics, cryptographic provenance, hard real-time operation, hardware independence, formal completeness, certification, or flight readiness.
