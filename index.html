Evidence boundary

Executable does not mean flight-certified.

Present: inspectable browser demonstrations, explicit authority topology, executable C state-machine logic, unit tests, randomized property testing, sanitizer CI, and a CBMC harness.

Not established: mission-valid thresholds, operational detector performance, cryptographic provenance, hard real-time guarantees, hardware isolation, certification credit, or a completed spacecraft safety case.

Evidence may reduce permission. It cannot manufacture authority.
