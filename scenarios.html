        Evidence boundary

        These are architecture demonstrations, not flight validation.

        Shown: mission-specific satellite authority mapping, authority separation, immediate demotion, unknown-evidence handling, Anchor survival invariants, bounded ground recovery, and explicit negative controls.

        Not shown: operational detector performance, complete causal models, certification, flight readiness, validated maneuver envelopes, sentinel safety, or proof that a fictional narrative maps directly to a real spacecraft.
